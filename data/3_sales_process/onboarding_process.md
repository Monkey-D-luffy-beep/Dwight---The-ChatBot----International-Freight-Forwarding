## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How long does onboarding take?
**A:** Standard onboarding can be completed in 1-2 business days once all documents are received. Credit account setup may take additional time for assessment.

### Q: Is there a minimum volume requirement?
**A:** No minimum volume requirement to start. We serve businesses of all sizes.

### Q: Can I track my shipments online?
**A:** Yes, through FreightJar you get real-time tracking and notifications for all shipments.

### Q: Who will be my contact person?
**A:** You'll be assigned a dedicated customer service representative as your primary contact.

### Q: Can I switch from standard to credit account later?
**A:** Yes, after establishing a relationship, you can apply for a credit account.
