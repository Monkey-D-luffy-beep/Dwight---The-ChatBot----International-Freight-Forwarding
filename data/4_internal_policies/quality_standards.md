# Tiger Logistics - Quality Standards & Service Levels

## Overview
This document outlines the quality standards and service level expectations for all Tiger Logistics operations. Adherence to these standards is essential for maintaining our reputation for excellence.

---

## Core Quality Principles

### Our Commitment
- **Reliability:** Deliver on our promises
- **Accuracy:** Get it right the first time
- **Responsiveness:** Act quickly and proactively
- **Professionalism:** Maintain high standards in all interactions
- **Continuous Improvement:** Always seek to do better

### Quality Policy
Tiger Logistics is committed to providing end-to-end logistics solutions that meet or exceed customer expectations through:
- Consistent service delivery
- Continuous process improvement
- Employee development
- Customer focus

## Process Quality Standards

### Quotation Process
- Complete cost breakdown
- Clear validity period
- Accurate transit times
- All terms and conditions included
- Professional formatting

### Booking Process
- Confirmation within 2 hours of space confirmation
- All details verified before confirmation
- Clear customer action items communicated
- Booking reference provided

### Documentation Process
- Compliance with all regulations
- Consistent information across documents
- Proper authorization and signatures
- Timely submission to authorities
- Accurate filing and records

### Shipment Execution
- Pre-shipment verification
- Milestone tracking and updates
- Exception management
- Delivery confirmation
- Post-delivery follow-up

---

## Communication Standards

### Professional Communication
- Clear and concise language
- Correct grammar and spelling
- Professional tone
- Timely responses
- Complete information

### Customer Updates
Mandatory updates at:
1. Booking confirmation
2. Cargo receipt at origin
3. Departure from origin
4. Any exceptions or delays
5. Arrival at destination
6. Customs clearance
7. Final delivery

### Issue Communication
- Acknowledge within SLA
- Explain situation clearly
- Provide resolution timeline
- Follow up until resolved
- Confirm customer satisfaction
