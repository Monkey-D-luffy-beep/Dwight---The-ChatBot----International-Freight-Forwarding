## Understanding Shipment Status

### Common Status Updates

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Booked** | Shipment has been booked and confirmed |
| **Picked Up** | Cargo collected from origin |
| **At Origin Port** | Arrived at departure port/airport |
| **In Transit** | Cargo is moving to destination |
| **At Destination Port** | Arrived at arrival port/airport |
| **Customs Clearance** | Undergoing customs processing |
| **Out for Delivery** | En route to final destination |
| **Delivered** | Successfully delivered to consignee |

### Additional Status Types
- **On Hold** - Awaiting documentation or clearance
- **Delayed** - Schedule change due to external factors
- **Exception** - Requires attention or action

## Service-Specific Tracking

### FCL Shipments
- Container-level tracking

- Consolidated shipment tracking
- CFS (Container Freight Station) status

### Air Freight
- Flight-level tracking
- MAWB/HAWB number tracking
- Airport-to-airport status
- Customs status at destination

### Cold Chain
- Temperature monitoring data (where applicable)
- Reefer container status
- Quality checkpoints

## Tracking Best Practices

1. **Save Your Reference Numbers**
   - Keep Bill of Lading/Airway Bill numbers accessible
   - Note your FreightJar booking reference

2. **Set Up Notifications**
   - Enable email/SMS alerts in FreightJar
   - Stay informed of critical milestones

3. **Monitor Proactively**
   - Check status regularly for time-sensitive cargo
   - Contact support early if you notice delays

4. **Document Everything**
   - Screenshot important status updates
   - Maintain records for claims if needed

## Troubleshooting

### "My shipment shows no updates"
- Verify the tracking number is correct
- LCL shipments may have delayed updates during consolidation
- Contact support if no updates for 48+ hours

### "Status shows 'On Hold'"
- Usually indicates documentation requirements
- Contact your assigned representative
- Check if customs documents are complete

### "Delivery is delayed"
- Weather, port congestion, and other factors can cause delays
- Check for updated ETA in FreightJar
- Contact support for revised timelines
