## Pricing Structure

### Rate Components
- **Air Freight Rate:** Based on chargeable weight
- **Fuel Surcharge:** Variable based on fuel prices
- **Security Surcharge:** Mandatory screening fees
- **Origin Charges:** Handling, documentation
- **Destination Charges:** Handling, delivery
- **Customs Fees:** Based on cargo value and type

### Weight Calculation
Chargeable Weight = Higher of:
- **Actual Weight** (in kg)
- **Volumetric Weight** (L × W × H in cm ÷ 6000)

### Factors Affecting Cost
- Origin and destination airports
- Cargo weight and dimensions
- Commodity type
- Urgency level
- Special handling requirements
- Current demand and capacity
