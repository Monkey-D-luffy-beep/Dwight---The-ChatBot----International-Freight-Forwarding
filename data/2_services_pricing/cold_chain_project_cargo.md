# Tiger Logistics - Cold Chain & Project Logistics Services

## Cold Chain Logistics

### Overview
Tiger Logistics is a specialist in handling international cargo that requires temperature-controlled conditions. Our specialized team understands the temperature-sensitivity of cargo and ensures climate-controlled cold chain transportation, warehousing, and shipping throughout the journey.

**Tagline:** *"Climate Control for Your Shipment"*

- Organic produce

#### Pharmaceuticals & Healthcare

- Biologics

#### FMCG Products

#### Commodities

- Industrial materials

### Service Offerings

| Service | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Reefer Containers | 20' and 40' refrigerated containers |
| Temperature Monitoring | Real-time temperature tracking |
| Cold Storage | Warehousing at origin/destination |
| Controlled Trucks | Temperature-maintained road transport |

### Temperature Ranges
- **Frozen:** -18°C to -25°C
- **Chilled:** +2°C to +8°C
- **Controlled Ambient:** +15°C to +25°C

## Project & Defence Logistics

### Overview
Tiger Logistics is an industry leader in handling oversized and heavy-lift project cargo. With tailored project plans and designated project managers, we have proven expertise in executing complex project logistics with precision.

**Tagline:** *"Project Logistics - Out of the Box Solutions"*

### Core Capabilities

#### Cargo Types
- **ODC (Over Dimensional Cargo):** Exceeds standard dimensions
- **Heavy-Lift:** Extremely heavy items requiring special equipment
- **Break Bulk:** Large items that can't be containerized
- **Industrial Machinery:** Factory equipment, turbines, generators
- **Prefabricated Structures:** Building components

#### Services

| Service | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Route Surveys | Feasibility studies for movement |
| Transport Planning | Multi-modal route optimization |
| Special Equipment | Cranes, low-bed trailers, etc. |
| ISO Tanks | Liquid cargo in tank containers |
| On-site Management | Supervision during loading/unloading |
| Project Management | Dedicated manager for each project |

### Industries Served
- **Aviation:** Aircraft parts, engines, equipment
- **Automobile:** Production lines, assembly units
- **Manufacturing:** Heavy machinery, industrial equipment
- **Defence:** Military equipment, vehicles, aircraft
- **Energy/Oil & Refinery:** Rigs, pipelines, equipment
- **Healthcare:** Medical equipment, imaging machines
- **Construction/Infrastructure:** Prefab structures, heavy materials
- **Telecom:** Towers, equipment, infrastructure

### Project Execution Approach
1. **Consultation:** Understand cargo and requirements
2. **Route Survey:** Assess feasibility and obstacles
3. **Planning:** Develop detailed execution plan
4. **Permissions:** Obtain necessary approvals
5. **Equipment:** Arrange specialized equipment
6. **Execution:** Supervised transport operation
7. **Delivery:** Safe placement at destination
8. **Documentation:** Complete handover
