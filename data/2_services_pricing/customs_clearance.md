## Clearance Process

### Import Clearance Steps
1. **Document Collection:** Gather all required documents
2. **Classification:** HS code determination
3. **Assessment:** Duty calculation
4. **Filing:** Bill of Entry submission
5. **Examination:** Physical/document check (if required)
6. **Duty Payment:** Process payment
7. **Out of Charge:** Release cargo
8. **Delivery:** Coordinate final delivery

### Export Clearance Steps
1. **Documentation:** Prepare shipping documents
2. **Filing:** Shipping Bill submission
3. **Examination:** LEO (Let Export Order) process
4. **Carting:** Move to port/airport
5. **Loading:** Cargo loading onto vessel/aircraft
6. **EGM Filing:** Final export confirmation
